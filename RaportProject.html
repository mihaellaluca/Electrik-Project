Project Description

Electrik project is a mash-up web application meant for clients to buy electrical products from different producers, to find easier charging stations for their products, and also Electrik will provide a method for paying energy invoices. Clients can subscribe to their favourite products and will be notified if there are any specific changes on them. Admins will be able see statistics based on sells, stocks and users in time.

Use Case:

    Client:
  • As a user, I will be able to login/register.
  • As a user, I will be able to view my profile and bills of my buyed products.
  • As a user, I will be able to view my favourites products.
  • As a user, I will be able to view a list of all products and filter them based on categories.
  • As a user, I will be able to buy products.
  • As a user, I will be notified about products to which I subscribed.
  • As a user, I will be able to view a map with all the charging stations and to filter them based on product categories.

    • Admin:
  • As an admin, I will be able to login.
  • As an admin, I will be able to view statistics based on sells on the website.

Database

Each microservice will have its own database.

    Users Database:
  • user_id (primary key)
  • user_rights
  • email
  • first_name
  • last_name
  • pass hash
  • favorite_products[products_ids]
    Products Database:
  • products_id (primary key)
  • name
  • model
  • description
  • stock
  • price
  • producer
  • charging_stations_coordinates
    Invoices Database:
  • invoice_id (primary key)
  • user_id
  • product_id
